The Myth of Beanie Babies

Modeled after the ferocious creature known as the bear, beanie babies are toys that for some weird reason were exceedingly popular in the 90's. They came in a bunch of different colors and styles but they were all pretty terrible. Some people collected them and some of them became obsessed, leading to many deaths across the country.
